About

Versatile.

Musical.

Jacques Smith II is a professional drummer and producer from Louisville, Kentucky. A graduate of Berklee College of Music, he studied Professional Music with concentrations in Performance, Music Business, and Production & Engineering.

Comfortable across genres including funk, gospel, pop, blues, rock, jazz, country, and hip hop, Jacques brings a versatile, musical approach to every stage and studio he steps into. Now based in New York City, he tours, performs, produces, writes, and works as a sought-after session musician.
